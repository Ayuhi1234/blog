Built after reading *Deep Work* and getting frustrated with how bloated every existing Pomodoro app had become. This one does exactly one thing: starts a timer, tracks the session, and gets out of the way.

## Why archived

Superseded by a simpler system (a physical timer and a paper log) that I ended up preferring. The app still works, but I'm not actively maintaining it — keeping it public as a reference for anyone building their own menu bar utilities in SwiftUI.
