0waste started as a spreadsheet and a WhatsApp group before it was a codebase. I'm building it in public — the full story of why I quit my job for this is [here](/blog/quit-job-for-0waste).

## The problem

Businesses that want to switch to sustainable packaging have no reliable way to find, compare, or trust suppliers. It's a fragmented market where good actors and greenwashers look identical from the outside, and the businesses most motivated to do the right thing have the least time to do the research themselves.

## The approach

Manual verification first, tooling second. Every supplier on the platform is vetted before automated matching does any of the work — trust is the actual product in the early stages, and trust doesn't scale by skipping the manual step.

## Where it stands

Live, with a growing base of verified suppliers and a bulk-order flow that's replaced the original WhatsApp-and-spreadsheet process entirely.
