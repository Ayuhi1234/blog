Ek Book Jisne Meri Soch Badal Di – The Alchemist

Kabhi kabhi ek book sirf story nahi hoti, woh aapki thinking change kar deti hai. Mere liye woh book hai The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho.

Jab maine ye book start ki thi, mujhe laga ye ek shepherd ki treasure hunt ki story hogi. Lekin jaise-jaise main padhte gaya, mujhe realize hua ki asli treasure gold ya money nahi tha—balki woh lessons the jo usne apni journey mein seekhe.

Sabse bada lesson jo mujhe mila:
 Life mein perfect time ka wait mat kro. Bass start karo
Hume mein se bahut log isliye shuru nahi krte kyuki humne lgta hai ki abhi hu read nhi hain.Main bhi aisa hi sochti thi .Lekin is book ne sikhaya ki clarity planning se nahi, action lene se aati hai.

Ek aur lesson jo mujhe bhut pasand aaya:
Fear of failure , failure se bhi zyada dangerous hota hai

Kai baar hum fail isliye nhi hote kyuki hum capable nahi hain.HUm fail isliye hote hai kyuki hum try hi nhi krte.

Book mein shepherd apni journey ke dauran bahut baar problems face karta hai. Kabhi paise chori ho jaate hain, kabhi plans change ho jaate hain, kabhi usse lagta hai ki sab khatam ho gaya. Lekin baad mein wahi experiences uski sabse badi strength ban jaate hain.

Ye lesson sirf books mein nahi, real life mein bhi true hai.

Aaj jab main apni life dekhti hoon, toh realize hota hai ki jo difficult phases the, unhone hi mujhe sabse zyada grow karaya.

Ek aur cheez jo is book ne mujhe sikhayi:

Destination se zyada important journey hoti hai.

Hum sab apne goals ke peeche bhaagte rehte hain—job, startup, promotion ya success. Lekin journey ke dauran jo skills, experiences aur mindset develop hota hai, wahi asli achievement hoti hai.

Isi wajah se maine decide kiya ki main apni learning ko document karungi. Main expert nahi hoon, lekin har din kuch naya seekhti hoon. Aur shayad meri learning kisi aur ke bhi kaam aa jaaye.
